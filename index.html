Malaga, Spain

Malaga is a municipality of Spain, capital of the Province of Málaga, in the autonomous community of Andalusia. It lies in Southern Iberia on the Costa del Sol ("Coast of the Sun") of the Mediterranean, primarily in the left bank of the Guadalhorce. Málaga's history spans about 2,800 years, making it one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in Western Europe.
